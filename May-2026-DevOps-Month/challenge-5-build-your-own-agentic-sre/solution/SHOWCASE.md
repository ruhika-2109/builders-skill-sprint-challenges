# Showcase — Challenge 5 (for facilitators)

There's no single answer — this challenge is open-ended. Use this page to record the best submissions and give a shoutout.

## What a strong entry looks like
- An **original** break (not a copy of challenges 1–4).
- The agent **actually investigated** it and gave a sensible root cause.
- A clear write-up + screenshot.
- Bonus: a Slack integration, a runbook, or a fix written with a coding assistant like Kiro.

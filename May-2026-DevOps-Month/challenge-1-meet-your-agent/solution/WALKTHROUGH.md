# Solution — Challenge 1: Meet Your Agent

> Try it yourself first!

There's no "right answer" here — the goal is just to chat with the agent and confirm your free trial works.

## What good looks like
- You opened the DevOps Agent web app.
- You asked a plain-English question in Chat.
- The agent replied with a real answer about your account (an inventory, a health summary, or "no resources found").

## If the agent sees nothing
Your account might be empty, or the Agent Space isn't connected to it.
- Upload `../../challenge-2-first-investigation/template.yaml` in CloudFormation to create one small resource, then ask again.
- Or recheck the account connection in [SETUP.md](../../SETUP.md) Step 3.

## Why this challenge exists
Before we break things in later challenges, you need to (1) confirm the agent is on and billing-safe, and (2) get comfortable just *talking* to it. That's the whole point.
